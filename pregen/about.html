About



Luciano Caravaggio

Head Instructor


Luc (“Looch”) enjoyed 4 successful years as a goaltender with NCAA Div. 1 Michigan Technological University where he played in over 95 games and was an All-WCHA Honorable Mention his sophomore season. Luc was drafted by the New Jersey Devils in the sixth round of the 1994 NHL Entry Draft. Following his college career, Luc played professionally for six full seasons and appeared in over 230 games.

Luc graduated from Michigan Tech with a B.S. in Mathematics and has spent several seasons coaching minor hockey in the GTA.
